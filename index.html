About Me

In my role as a Senior Software Engineer at Barclays, I thrive on crafting high-volume credit card domain APIs that elevate customer experiences. I design and implement robust, low‑latency RESTful services and backend workflows, working closely with product and operations teams to deliver measurable improvements in performance and user satisfaction.

With over five years in full‑stack development, my core expertise lies in backend technologies and distributed systems. I build scalable systems using tools like Java/Spring Boot and Python/Django, applying multithreading, caching, and solid system design to improve throughput, maintainability, and operational efficiency.

I hold a B.E. in Computer Science Engineering from Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Pune. During my studies I focused on operating systems, machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing—foundations that guide both my research and engineering work today.

I have co‑authored two peer‑reviewed papers and led projects that translate ML research into applied systems, including a hybrid BiLSTM + modified SIR model for hospital forecasting and Eatiza, a CNN‑based food and nutrition analysis platform.

I enjoy competitive problem solving and have won first prize at a national‑level hackathon and additional organization‑level hackathons. Outside of engineering, I sketch and read regularly—activities that keep my creative thinking sharp and provide balance.
